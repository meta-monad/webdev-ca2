How to play

Are you confused about playing the game? This page will explain the controls and mechanics of this game.

Controls

Control Description
Right Click Iterate cursor modes
Plus key Zooms in on the map
Minus key Zooms out of the map
Arrow keys Move the map's view

Mechanics

Cursor modes

The cursor serves as a multi-purpose tool in this videogame. By default, it start off in Move mode with a yellow outline. In this move, clicks will instruct the player character to find a way to the location specified. In Attack mode, the cursor turns red as a click now becomes an attack. Be careful with this one. Some enemies might be sleeping. And last, but not least, the Info mode with its blue highlight lets you learn about the world around you, by providing a description of the objects you click on.

Additionally, due to technical limitations, switching between weapons is done by cycling the cursor modes once.

Player attributes

The 3 core player attributes lets you choose your own playstyle. They influence a variety of aspects in the game from movement speed to combat. At a maximum of 15 points in total, each attribute ranges from 1 to 10. Let's break them down individually.

  • Endurance: How tough your character is. It determines how much damage can you soak up before passing out.
  • Perception: How good is the vision of your character. Higher perception makes your aim better with lower chances of a miss. It may also help your turn come sooner in combat cycles.
  • Agility: With a more agile character, you are able to take more action in combat.

Combat

Combat is a cycle of every participant, which repeats until all but one are dead. During combat, the order is determined by a combat score which is calculated based on the perception attribute. During each turn, every entity and the player have a set number of actions alloted to them. Moving 1 tile takes up 1 action. Attacking (whether successful or not) takes up 2. During combat, a weapon might miss, make a hit, or even make a critical hit (up to double the damage).
